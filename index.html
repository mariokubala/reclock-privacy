Privacy Policy for Simple ReClock

Last updated: October 17, 2024

Information Collection and Use

Simple ReClock does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.

Camera Permission

This app requires camera permission to record video. All recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your camera or recordings.

Audio Recording Permission

This app requires microphone permission to record audio with video. Audio is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All video recordings are saved directly to your device's Camera folder. No data is transmitted to any external servers.
